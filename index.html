   The Problem

To control the spread of Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19), screening large numbers of suspected cases for appropriate quarantine and treatment is a priority. Pathogenic laboratory testing such as RT-PCR is the diagnostic gold standard but it is time-consuming and requires significantly advanced biotech infrastructure. Majority of young individuals will be asymptomatic at least for five to six days or even two weeks which makes selective diagnosis and infection control very hard.

   The Solutions

CovidSense is a fully automatic framework under development which could detect COVID-19 primarily using coughing patterns along with sounds of their voice and their breathing. Well also extract visual features from volumetric chest CT exams or chest X-ray images to develop our algorithm.
CovidSense will be an integrated platform that will in future include a dedicated mobile app and an loT based wearable, specifically a nanoparticle-based diagnostic skin patch which can detect the presence of biomarkers related to the SARS CoV 2 infection in sweat, sebum or skin transudate.

   Why CovidCough?

  • CovidSense will be the most accurate Al-based COVID-19 screening tool as it uses multiple parameters to pinpoint high-risk individuals.
  • The only Al platform which can detect completely asymptomatic carriers of SARS CoV-2 virus by monitoring their skin secretions.
  • Low cost highly efficient sentinel surveillance tool for the governments of developing countries and international health organisations.
  • Can be used for Infection prevention, early diagnosis and prognosis monitoring of the community.

   Your Privacy


Why do we need the cough sound data from you?

About 65% of COVID-19 patients may develop a cough, either productive or dry during the period of infection. Cough sounds and their pattern can give vital information about the state of your respiratory system. Most acute or subacute coughs caused by viral respiratory tract infections have some specific pattern. We want to study the acoustic signatures of coughs to try and find the early signs of COVID-19.

Why do we need the diagnostic radiography based data from you?

Chest computed tomography severity score (CT.SS) along with other parameters are being used to evaluate pulmonary involvement in COVID-19 patients. A diagnostic image of your chest, whether CT or simple X-Ray image can reveal many details. Our Al tool could help to identify COVID-19 from other viral pneumonia and bacterial pneumonia by analysing CT abnormalities including ground-glass opacities.

Why do we need your COVID-19 test results? Who will have access to it?

Although we only need the lab reports of COVID-19 positive participants, anyone can submit the report if they have been tested. This is to confirm the SARS Co V -2 infection. We recommend you to submit a redacted version of your lab report (with private information struck out/blacked out or hidden). The lab reports will only be accessible to select people from the core research team, with strict access control and access logging.

Where will we store your data?

The data that you entered will be stored in a HIPAA-secure cloud platform after encryption, with access control and access logging.

Will any contact information you enter will be shared with anyone?

We are not asking for any kind of contact information from you. Even if your contact information is present in any of the documents you upload, we will anonymise those data as we notice the same.
